Travel

Kolkata

The Pinnacle of my Travelling experience is without a doubt the week i spent in Kolkata as a 16 year old. Traveling as part of the Hope foundation this trip was certainly an eye opener for me. The venture entailed a variety of mind blowing endeavours to crowded orphanages all the way to destitute slums. Never have i been so changed by an experience. Upon returning i remain a part of the hope foundation and have since joined many other charitable organisations such as the Lourdes Pilgrimage.

Lourdes

Every year my local secondary school sends two students as helpers with local pilgrims to Lourdes. I was lucky enough to be selected and was ecstatic when one of my friends was selected with me. Once again this trip was quite eye opening as I saw people on their final years find solace in this spectacular place. This was definitely a moving and inspiring trip as the majority was spent helping and offering companionship too the pilgrims. The experience and wisdom received was certainly worth the long hours!

Cairo

Truly a terrific tourist destination, Cairo was an incredible choice due to its rich history. There was so much to do and even more left undone. Sightseeing alone could fill a tourists needs for the entire two weeks. One of the most incredible sights i have ever seen has to be the high rising pyramid of Giza breaking the skyline. One can simply not enjoy Egypt enough and i wish to return
